> 1\. To appear to change, to transform one's self, to cause illusions, to alter an object by magic\. 2\. To be mistaken, err, to make blunders\. 3\. To separate, sort, discriminate (the good from the bad, truth from falsehood) (Jä) CD\. For a false meaning to arise, manifest to the mind or senses DS\. To manifest magically, to move TC\.
