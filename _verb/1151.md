`Voluntary` `Erg-Abs`
> To rob, run away with, to deprive of CD\.
 To rob, snatch, grab, to attract DK\.
 1\.
 To take something by force that which had not been given\.
 2\.
 To take by force for oneself\.
 3\.
 To bring to mind DS\.
 To take, assemble by force TC\.
