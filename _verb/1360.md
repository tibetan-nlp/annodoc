`Involuntary` `Abs-Obl`
> 1\.
 To become ripe, to ripen\.
 2\.
 To be converted CD\.
 To age, grow old, to become tamed, to be peaceful minded DK\.
 1\.
 For a fruit of a fruit tree or seeds to become ready\.
 2\.
 To have the power to think about a meaning\.
 3\.
 To reach a year of age\.
 4\.
 To bear fruit, fructify DS\.
 To reach a measure, to do well established TC\.
