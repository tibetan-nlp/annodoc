`Involuntary` `Abs-Obl`
> (used with la) To desire, to long for, to be attached to, to have affection for CD\.
 To have attachment, to long for DK\.
 1\.
 To crave, to cherish\.
 2\.
 (of a question &c\.
) To be hard to answer DS\.
 To like, to love TC\.
