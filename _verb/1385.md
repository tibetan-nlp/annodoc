`Voluntary` `Erg-Abs`
> To build, to erect CD\.
 To wall up, to pile up, to build, erect DK\.
 1\.
 To make higher by placing one (stone &c\.
) on top of another\.
 2\.
 To make, erect (a castle wall &c\.
) DS\.
 To build a wall TC\.
