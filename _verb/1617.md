> (in Old Tibetan contracts) To forfeit, be confiscated (Takeuchi 1995: 185)\. To shrink, subside, cause diminution, to be displaced CD\. To diminish, vanish, disappear DK\. 1\. To disappear, to become nothing\. 2\. To suffer a lose DS\. To vanish, disappear, become invisible TC\.

* Volition: _Involuntary_
* Arguments: _Abs-Obl_
