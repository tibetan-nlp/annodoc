`Voluntary` `Erg-Abs`
> 1\.
 To hold, to take hold of, to keep, to bear\.
 2\.
 To carry, wear\.
 3\.
 To have, to assume (e\.
g\.
 body of a goddess)\.
 4\.
 To bite, bark at CD\.
 1\.
 To hold with the hand\.
 2\.
 To hold in mind without forgetting DS\.
 To hold, to grasp to seize, to maintain, to assume\.
 2\.
 To keep in mind, to retain TC\.
