`VoluntaryInvoluntary` `Erg-Abs`
> To appear to change, to transform one's self, to cause illusions, to alter an object by magic CD\.
 To transform, to perform miracles, to exhibit a miracle, to incarnate DK\.
 To change (of speech by superstition or divine stories) DS\.
 To manifest (of the body of Buddha which arises because of Buddhist teachings) TC\.
