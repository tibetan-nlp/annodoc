`Involuntary` `Abs-Obl`
> 1\.
 (with terminative of the substantive) To know, understand\.
 2\.
 (with terminative of the infinitive) To know that, to perceive CD\.
 To understand, to comprehend, to apprehend DK\.
 To see with the eyes DS\.
 1\.
 To see\.
 2\.
 To know, to understand TC\.
