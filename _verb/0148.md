`Involuntary` `Abs`
> (in Medicine) To boil CD\.

 2\.
 To feel anxiety or to be agitated\.
 3\.
 To be active in service, to be an obedient servant DK\.
 For bubbles to arise from within a liquid which has arrived at a certain temperature DS\.
