`Voluntary` `Erg-Abs`
> To bind, fasten, attach, to tie round CD\.
 To name, to put on, to wear ornaments, to hang, suspend from, to make a false accusation DK\.
 1\.
 To tie, link (rope &c\.
)\.
 2\.
 To name something\.
 3\.
 To speak about nothing (a fault, mistake &c\.
)\.
 4\.
 To establish benefit DS\.
 To tie, bind TC\.
