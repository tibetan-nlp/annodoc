`Voluntary` `Abs-Obl`
> 1\.
 To attain to, to reach\.
 2\.
 To touch, to come in contact with\.
 3\.
 (especially in West) To feel, to perceive CD\.
 To touch, to reach, to attain to DK\.
 To touch, contact directly DS\.
 To touch, contact, attain to, reach TC\.
