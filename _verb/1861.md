`Voluntary` 
> 1\.
 To do a thing secretly, to conceal\.
 2\.
 To be careful, to watch CD\.
 To hide, to keep a secret DK\.
 To hide, to make unknown DS\.
 To hide, to obfuscate to another TC\.
