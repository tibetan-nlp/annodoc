`Voluntary` 
> To turn round, turn about, to turn upside down, turn inside out CD\.
 1\.
 To return, to send back, give back\.
 2\.
 To turn over, to reverse DK\.
 1\.
 To reverse up and down, top and bottom\.
 2\.
 To change the direction something was going\.
 3\.
 To plough up, dig up (land &c\.
)\.
 4\.
 To give something back DS\.
 1\.
 To newly plough up (uncultivated land &c\.
) interchanging top and bottom, inside and outside\.
 2\.
 To reverse TC\.
