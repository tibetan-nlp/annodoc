`Involuntary` `Abs-Obl`
> 1\.
 To be peaceful, calm, to be pacified, be appeased, to settle be allayed, assuaged &c\.
 2\.
 To be wise, dispassionate CD\.
 To be, become peaceful, to subdue, make without DK\.
 To disappear DS\.
 To diminish, fade away TC\.
