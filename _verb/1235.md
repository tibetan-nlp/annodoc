`Involuntary` 
> 1\.
 To be wiped out, effaced\.
 2\.
 To fall off (of the hair) (Jä) CD\.
 To disappear one's hair by falling off [sic], to disappear or vanish, to be effaced, be wiped off DK\.
 For hair to fall off of a creature's body DS\.
 To come out (for head hair, body hair &c) TC\.
