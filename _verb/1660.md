`Involuntary` `Abs`
> To be the time or right moment for anything, to be proper, just right, even CD\.
 To be suitable, to suit DK\.
 1\.
 To conform, comply, to be fitting, appropriate\.
 2\.
 For a fixed, determined time to arrive DS\.
 1\.
 To be appropriate, fitting, conforming\.
 2\.
 To arrive at a time TC\.
