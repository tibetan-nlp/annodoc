`Involuntary` `Abs-Obl`
> To be stopped; to be halted CD\.
 1\.
 To speak unclearly, to have difficulty speaking\.
 2\.
 For something to be unable to be free from inside (e\.
g\.
 a hole)\.
 3\.
 For a continuum to break off DS\.
 To be blocked, to be obstructed TC\.
