> To measure, to apportion, to fix CD\. To estimate, to ascertain, to confirm DK\. 1\. To asses, establish a fact which is not know having established the causes for its outward signs which are known\. 2\. To analyze, investigate DS\. To measure, asses, investigate a hidden phenomenon TC\.
