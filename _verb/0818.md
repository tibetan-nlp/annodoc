`Voluntary` 
> 1\.
 To be strewn, scattered\.
 2\.
 To be dispersed, desiccated, burst (of a gun) ('thor) CD\.
 1 To scatter, strew, spread over (Moñ)\.
 2\.
 To cast, throw, throw out (gtor) CD\.
 To spread over, strew, scatter DK\.
 To spread out, separate individually\.
 2\.
 To throw to the ground DS\.
 To scatter, throw in small bits\.
 2\.
 To tear apart, destroy TC\.
