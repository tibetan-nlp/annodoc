`Involuntary` `Abs-Obl`
> (with la or nang du) To be lost (in something else), to melt down, be dissolved in, vanish CD\.
 To merge into DK\.
 To mix into an un-differentiable essence DS\.
 To sink, dissolve, vanish TC\.

### Dependencies (N = 3)
* `arg1` 2
* `arg2:hon` 1
* `aux` 1
* `obl:arg` 1
