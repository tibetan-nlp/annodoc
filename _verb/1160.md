> (archaic) To express, to mutter CD\. 1\. To ask (a god, sovereign)\. 2\. To give, offer (to a god, sovereign)\. 3\. (for a god, sovereign) To receive, obtain, take\. 4\. (probable) To murmur, recite (charms, prayers)\. 5\. (probable) To invoke, summon, call (Uray 1964)\. To express, recite, mutter DK\.
