> 1\.
 To find, get, obtain (especially in West and Sikkim unacceptable in classical)\.
 2\.
 To get, obtain, procure, receive (in classical)\.
 3\.
 To become CD\.
 To obtain, get DK\.
 1\.
 To come in hand\.
 2\.
 To take, seize\.
 3\.
 To be worthy, suitable, conform to, comply with DS\.
 To find TC\.
