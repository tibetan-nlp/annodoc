> To toss about, to swirl, to entwine, be choked, suffocated CD\. To swirl (snow, dust, wind, water, &c\.), to be suffocated, choked, overflow DK\. 1\. To gather, mass, billow (dust, smell &c\.)\. 2\. To wheeze, breath with difficulty DS\. To be chaotic, growl, shake, disturbed TC\.
