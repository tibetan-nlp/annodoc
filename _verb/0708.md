`Voluntary` `Abs-Obl`
> To keep, hold, adhere to, lean on CD\.
 To base on, depend on, rest upon DK\.
 To rely on, depend on DS\.
 1\.
 To rely on, use as a support\.
 2\.
 To stay, remain, live TC\.
