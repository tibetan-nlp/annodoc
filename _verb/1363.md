`VoluntaryInvoluntary` `Erg-Obl`
> To bless, wish, desire (others to be happy and prosperous)\.
 2\.
 (with terminative of the infinitive) To pray for CD\.
 To bless, wish, desire, admire, like DK\.
 To have a very good wish, hope DS\.
 To desire to obtain TC\.
