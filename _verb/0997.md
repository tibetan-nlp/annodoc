> 1\.
 To sit, to be placed, to exist, to be in force, to continue, exist\.
 2\.
 To reside, dwell, stay\.
 3\.
 To remain, continue, adhere CD\.
 To sit, situate, to have, to rest, value DK\.
 1\.
 To sit, stay, live\.
 2\.
 For things to exist in a place and time continuously between their appearance and destruction DS\.
