`Voluntary` `Erg-Abs`
> 1\.
 To open, to place apart, to unloose\.
 2\.
 (in West) To separate, to keep asunder, to disentangle CD\.
 To separate, to distinguish, to keep apart, open DK\.
 To open (a door &c\.

 1\.
 To separate, split\.
 2\.
 To undo, release TC\.
