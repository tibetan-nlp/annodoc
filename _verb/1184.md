`Voluntary` 
> To ransom, redeem (a pawn, pledge, or security) CD\.
 To buy back, redeem; to redeem [the] life of a man, animal by paying some money; to ransom DK\.
 (honorific) To buy DS\.
 To buy something previously relied upon, (particularly) to buy for a religious purpose TC\.
