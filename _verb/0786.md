`Involuntary` `Abs-Ass`
> To agree with, to be accordant, to be on par with CD\.
 To agree, to be in agreement, to become similar DK\.
 1\.
 To be alike, similar\.
 2\.
 To agree\.
 3\.
 To be alright, be harmonious\.
 4\.
 To help, assist, give aid DS\.
 To be friendly, harmonious TC\.
