`Voluntary` `Abs-Ass`
> To mingle, mix, to make unite, to conjoin, fasten together CD\.
 To combine, associate with, to join, fasten DK\.
 1\.
 To mix many different things together\.
 2\.
 To keep company with, to accompany DS\.
 1\.
 To keep company with, to accompany\.
 2\.
 To join, to arrange TC\.
