`Voluntary` `Erg-Abs`
> 1\.
 To cause to return, to drive back, repulse, expel, send back\.
 2\.
 To send to fetch something\.
 3\.
 To cause to turn, to divert (e\.
g\.
 the mind, attention) CD\.
 To turn out, to return, to get rid of DK\.
 To move back, return DS\.
 To stop, make to turn outside, drive away TC\.
