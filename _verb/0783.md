> 1\.
 To hear\.
 (originally had the sense of hearing at second hand, thus -) 2\.
 To hear of, to have word of, to understand CD\.
 To hear, to listen DK\.
 To perceive sound with the ear DS\.
 To listen, understand with the ears TC\.
