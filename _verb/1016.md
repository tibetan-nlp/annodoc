`Involuntary` `Abs-Obl`
> 1\.
 To emit light, to shine, be bright\.
 2\.
 To be seen, perceived, to show one's self, to appear CD\.
 To be visible, to come in view of, to appear, to come into sight DK\.
 To become an object of the mind or sense organs DS\.
 1\.
 To shine, rise, be clear, distinct, visible\.
 2\.
 To exist, remain TC\.
