`Voluntary` 
> To cry, weep CD\.
 To cry; weep DK\.
1\.
 To shed tears and make a noise of suffering because of pain, illness, or sadness\.
 2\.
 To make a noise like crying, sobbing DS\.
 To weep, to cry TC\.
