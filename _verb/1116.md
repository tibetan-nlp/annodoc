`Involuntary` `Abs-Obl`
> To change place, go, move oneself away, migrate CD\.
 To be transferred, to move from one place to another place, to change DK\.
 1\.
 To move from one place to another place\.
 2\.
 To die DS\.
 To go, to pass on TC\.
