`Involuntary` `Abs-Obl`
> 1\.
 To turn round, circumambulate\.
 2\.
 To be formed, perfected\.
 CD\.
 To rotate or turn round DK\.
 1\.
 To turn at a hub\.
 2\.
 To change direction\.
 3\.
 To come, to gather DS\.
 To revolve, to rotate, to spin, to go around, to elapse (of time) TC\.
