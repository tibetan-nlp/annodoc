`Involuntary` `Abs-Abl/Ela`
> 1\.
 To be freed, to be liberated\.
 2\.
 To escape, get through, be able, pass CD\.
 To be freed, liberated, to get through, cross a bar DK\.
 To become free, to escape, slip away DS, TC\.
