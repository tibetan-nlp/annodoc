`Involuntary` `Obl-Abs`
> To find, discover, acquire, meet with, get CD\.
 1\.
 Having searched, to acquire at hand\.
 2\.
 To newly know an unknown meaning DS\.
 To procure, to get, to find DK\.
 To find, to discover, to obtain, to get TC\.
