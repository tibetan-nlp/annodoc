`Voluntary` `Erg-Abs`
> (Rdo\.
 46) To destroy, to devastate, to devour, to abolish, to do away with, dissolve (an encampment) CD\.
 To destroy, break into pieces DK\.
 1\.
 To tear down\.
 2\.
 To not adhere to, not support; to destroy, ruin, annihilate: 3\.
 (dialect) To undo, untie DS\.
 To destroy, exterminate, demolish TC\.
