`Involuntary` 
> To dream CD\.
 To have a dream, to dream DK\.
 When after having gone to sleep, for various appearances to arise because of conditions of the brain due to outer conditions or by the power of various conditions within and without the body DS\.
 For a dream to come about TC\.
