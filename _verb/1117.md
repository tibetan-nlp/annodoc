`Involuntary` `Abs-Obl`
> To strike, to affect, influence, touch CD\.
 To hit, strike DK\.
 1\.
 To hit on top\.
 2\.
 To come on top of the meaning of a verbal expression, to hurt, to offend someone\.
 3\.
 To get sick, be ill DS\.
 To hit, strike TC\.
