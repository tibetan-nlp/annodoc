`Involuntary` `Abs-Obl`
> To be complete, full, entire CD\.
 To be completed, the entire, number of requirements, to be completed the entire body of something [sic] DK\.
 For a number or measure to be complete, for all pieces to come together, be assembled DS\.
 For a number, measure to be complete, come together TC\.
