`Voluntary` 
> To feed, to give to eat and drink CD\.
 To give food from mouth to mouth (as a mother feeds her baby); to give or feed DK\.
 To give food to, feed (e\.
g\.
 a young child) DS\.
 To feed, to nourish, to spoon feed TC\.
