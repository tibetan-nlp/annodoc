> 1\.
 To give, grant, bestow, confer\.
 2\.
 To restore CD\.

 (honorific) To give DS\.
 1\.
 (honorific) To give, do\.
 2\.
 To begin an action TC\.
 (mchid stsal pa) To give a letter (from someone higher to lower) (Takeuchi 1990b: 178-183)\.
