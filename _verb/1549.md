`Voluntary` `Abs-Obl`
> (honorific) 1\.
 To sit\.
 2\.
 To dwell, reside\.
 3\.
 To remain, stay, exit\.
 4\.
 (of books) To be recorded in, to be contained CD\.
 (honorific) To sit, remain, live DS\.
 (honorific) To stay, sit, remain TC\.
