`Voluntary` 
> (archaic) 1\.
 To adjust exactly, adjust in a very accurate manner\.
 2\.
 To pray earnestly (in casting lots and in divination)\.
 3\.
 To grind, polish, rub, file CD\.

 2\.
 To make clean by sweeping\.
