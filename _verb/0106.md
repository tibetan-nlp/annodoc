`Abs-Obl`
> To find, get, earn, draw CD\.
To calm down; to bend down; to fall asleep; to valued; to be worth DK\.
To be the price of (something) DS\.
 1\.
 To turn into, to change, to become\.
 2\.
 To get, to make, to find TC\.
