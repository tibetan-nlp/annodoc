`Involuntary` `Abs`
> 1\.
 To catch fire, to become ignited, to blaze\.
 2\.
 (in reference to the passions) To glare, burn with wrath\.
 3\.
 To burst forth into bloom, to blossom, to blare forth into fame, to become notorious CD\.
 To blaze up, to catch fire, to increase DK\.
 1\.
 For tongues of fire to raise from a burning thing\.
 2\.
 To spread (of light)\.
 3\.
 To spread, grow DS\.

 To scold TC\.
