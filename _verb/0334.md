`Voluntary` 
> To unravel, to make loose, to set free, to unfasten CD\.
 To loosen; to set free from bondage DK\.
1\.
 (for a knot, tied rope &c\.
) To unravel, unwind\.
 2\.
 (for a higher ranking [person]) To allow, approve DS\.
1\.
 To untie, to unravel, to liberate, to release, to unfasten\.
 2\.
 To comment on, to elucidate TC\.
