`Involuntary` 
> To stroke, caress CD\.
 To advise on religious practice, to give advice at the time of admission into the higher order of Buddhist monk-hood\.
 To contain, subsume [TC]\.
 1\.
 To be soft, smooth, quiet\.
 2\.
 To cool down, become cold (Hoshi 2003)\.
