`Voluntary` `Erg-Abs`
> (used with terminative case of the infinitive) 1\.
 To set about, to be on the point, to prepare for\.
 2\.
 To brandish CD\.
 To raise something for hitting, to prepare, to give DK\.
 To aim for a goal DS\.
 To start, begin, prepare TC\.
