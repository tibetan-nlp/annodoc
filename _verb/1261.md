`VoluntaryInvoluntary` `Abs-Ass`
> To be separated from, disconnected CD\.
 To part, to be separate DK\.
 1\.
 To divide, separate\.
 2\.
 To lack, not exist DS\.
 To separate, scatter individually\.
 2\.
 To purify, be rid of TC\.
