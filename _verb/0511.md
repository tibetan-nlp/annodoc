`Involuntary` `Abs-Obl`
> 1\.
 To cut into pieces, to decay, to break off, separate, to break asunder\.
 2\.
 To cease, end, stop, die CD\.
 To be broken off, be cut off; to discontinue; to be tired DK\.
 1\.
 For a long thing to break into pieces\.
 2\.
 To separate into many sections\.
 3\.
 To be omitted, discarded\.
 4\.
 To degenerate, decay, cease to exit DS\.
 To be interrupted, to be broken, to be cut off\.
 2\.
 To be incomplete, to be missing TC\.
