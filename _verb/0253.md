`Involuntary` `Abs-Obl`
> (honorific) 1\.
 To rejoice, to be glad\.
 2\.
 To be pleased to; to choose CD\.
 To be delighted; to be happy, pleased; to rejoice DK\.
 (honorific) To become happy DS\.
 (honorific) To rejoice, to be happy, to be delighted TC\.
